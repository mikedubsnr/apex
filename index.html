About ApexVisa UK

What we do

ApexVisa UK is a visa risk intelligence platform. We analyse publicly available UKVI guidance, the Immigration Rules, and documented refusal patterns to help applicants understand the risks in their specific situation — before they submit an application and pay non-refundable visa fees.

Not Legal Advice

ApexVisa UK is not a law firm and does not provide regulated immigration advice. Nothing on this website constitutes legal advice. For regulated advice on your specific circumstances, consult an IAA-registered immigration adviser or an SRA-regulated solicitor.

Information Accuracy

All guides are based on publicly available UKVI published guidance and the Immigration Rules as of April 2026. Immigration law changes frequently. Always verify current requirements on GOV.UK before submitting any application. We accept no liability for decisions made based on information on this website.

Data Sources

Our guides reference and link directly to official GOV.UK sources including the Immigration Rules, Appendix FM, Appendix Skilled Worker, Appendix Student, Appendix V (Visitor), and Migration Statistics. All references are linked at the end of each guide for verification.
