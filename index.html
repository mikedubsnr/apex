ApexVisa UK is a structured risk assessment tool for UK visa applicants. It provides a questionnaire-based audit that generates a risk score and flagged areas based on your answers. The tool covers the Spouse/Partner, Skilled Worker, Visitor, Student, ILR, and citizenship visa routes.
